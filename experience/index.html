    Experience & Education

    Work Experience

    Senior Consulting Geophysicist — Ocean Floor Geophysics (Oceansight Group)

    May 2025 – Present | Gibsons, B.C., Canada

    • Provide senior geophysical expertise within Ocean Floor Geophysics, now operating as a division of the Oceansight Group following acquisition of Ocean Floor Geophysics Inc.

    Founder & Senior Consulting Geophysicist — Ocean Floor Geophysics Inc.

    January 2006 – May 2025 | Gibsons, B.C., Canada

    • Founded and led a company providing geophysical, AUV, and ROV services for deep-water mineral and gas hydrate exploration worldwide
    • Invented the OFG electromagnetic system (patent assigned); delivered EM, magnetic, gravity, and precision bathymetric mapping services using ROVs and AUVs
    • Served as chief scientist during acquisition of over 500 line-km of CSEM data in the Sea of Japan (2014); led two of the largest gas hydrate surveys ever conducted with the Scripps IOC Vulcan CSEM system (2015, 2017)

    Principal Geophysicist — Mira Geoscience, Advanced Geophysical Interpretation Centre (AGIC)

    January 2006 – February 2018 | Vancouver, B.C., Canada

    • Led geophysical interpretation and project management with a focus on computerized inversions of geophysical data
    • Performed IP 2D/3D, 3D magnetic, and 1D/2D/3D electromagnetic inversions using UBC-GIF algorithms; inverted VTEM and ZTEM aeromagnetic datasets
    • Responsible for technical quality and sign-off on major projects; served as lead geophysicist on individual interpretation projects

    Consulting Geophysicist — Geoscience BC

    July 2007 – July 2016 | Vancouver, B.C., Canada

    • Specified and technically supervised contracts for large, multi-year regional airborne gravity, electromagnetic, and magnetic data acquisition programs across British Columbia

    Chief Research Geophysicist — Barrick Gold

    January 2005 – December 2005 | Vancouver, B.C., Canada

    • Coordinated the transfer of Placer Dome Inc. geophysical assets to Barrick Gold following Barrick's acquisition of PDI

    Chief Geophysicist — Placer Dome Inc.

    May 1991 – January 2005 | Vancouver, B.C., Canada

    • Led worldwide minerals exploration and provided technical supervision of geophysicists and remote sensing specialists across PDI's 22 operating gold mines
    • Managed implementation of EM and electrical inversion algorithms; oversaw SWIR airborne hyperspectral scanner operations in the Kamchatka Peninsula, Russia
    • Provided consulting services for projects in Chile, Venezuela, and London (Placer Outokumpu); liased with university and government research groups

    Senior Geophysicist — Placer Dome Inc.

    1987 – May 1991 | Vancouver, B.C., Canada

    • Conducted worldwide minerals exploration with technical supervision of exploration geophysics and remote sensing
    • Provided consulting geophysical services in Tanzania, Guyana, Fiji, and Chile
    • Managed specialized computing services including image processing and electrical modelling for PDI's exploration geophysics group

    Research Geophysicist — Placer Development Ltd. (Technical Services Group)

    1981 – 1987 | Vancouver, B.C., Canada

    • Conducted minerals exploration and geophysical research worldwide, including gold exploration in Indonesia and Malaysia
    • Provided consulting services for PDUS in Nevada and Placer Exploration in Australia
    • Led research into image processing algorithms and coordinated image processing research across Placer Development and PEL

    Staff Geophysicist — Placer Development Ltd.

    1978 – 1980 | Toronto, Ont., Canada

    • Explored for Archean/Precambrian gold, VMS polymetallic, and uranium deposits in eastern Canada; also worked on Carlin-style gold and caldera-hosted mercury/uranium programs in Nevada
    • Worked on the initial implementation of GEOLOG, PDI's computerized geologic logging system

    Consulting Geophysicist — Marcopper Mining Corp.

    1975 – 1977 | Philippines

    • Conducted minerals exploration and computing consulting; developed theory and implemented work-index and recovery-sensitive cut-off grade definitions for mine grade control
    • Explored for porphyry copper and island-arc gold deposits throughout the Philippines

    Chief Geophysicist — Placer Exploration Pty. Ltd.

    1973 – 1974 | Sydney, Australia

    • Led minerals exploration in Australasia, targeting Mississippi Valley-style and SEDEX lead-zinc deposits and porphyry copper and gold deposits in PNG and the Pacific islands

    Staff Geophysicist — Placer Development Ltd.

    1970 – 1972 | Vancouver, B.C., Canada

    • Conducted field work and interpretation for copper porphyry, molybdenum porphyry, and skarn-style mineralization across the Western Cordillera
